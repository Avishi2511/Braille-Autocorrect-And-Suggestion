Keyboard Reference

QWERTY to Braille mapping

Left Hand
D Dot 1 (top-left)
W Dot 2 (middle-left)
Q Dot 3 (bottom-left)
Right Hand
K Dot 4 (top-right)
O Dot 5 (middle-right)
P Dot 6 (bottom-right)
How to use:
  • Type using the keyboard mapping
  • Use Tab or ↓/↑ to cycle through suggestions
  • Press Enter to accept a suggestion
  • Click on a suggestion to apply it
